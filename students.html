Campus and Activities

Immerse yourself in code while experiencing the real day to day culture of software engineers.

Part Time and Flexible Class Schedules

We're all about achieving work life balance, so let us know when you can attend. Our classes are designed to be small, in short-bursts of lectures, challenges and project-based learning. We want to give you the advantage to learn the material by asking those critical-thinking questions while learning the material.

"The Right Culture Fit"

Like most companies, they hire you not just based on your skill set and experience. Companies also weight in if you're the right culture fit. We'll provide you the tools to research about company values and goals and outlook and attend hackathons and networking meetups- learn about the existing workplace environment of software developers.

Weekly Project-Based Learning Coding Challenges

Build on weekly meaningful and purposeful projects before class, during class and after class under the wing of software developers. Every concept and line of code is intentional in the learning process and will contribute to the final project before graduation.

Intentional Learning Structure

Our class is structured in a engaging 3-hour instruction, coupled with project based learning from industry professionals who come from all walks of tech. We're focused on project based learning, and religiously follow a universal teaching design to layout the ground work for learning Front End Web Development.

Crack the Coding Interview

Learn the skills you'll need to slam dunk your next coding interview. You will receive the instruction and help required not only to think on your feet but solve real world problems that plague software developers today. You'll be able to talk your way through the white board to your offer letter.

Mentorship & Job Support

We feel that learning should NEVER be intimidating, so our instructors are patient, and willing to mentor and counsel with you through the entire way. Remember, at one point we were in your shoes, so we understand the sacrifices and challenges it takes to make a change.

Ready to to make the leap?

There is no shortage of bootcamps, but there is a great shortage for Front End Developers! Our track ensures gaining the technical skills that you need to be a developer AND also learning the soft skills to get your foot in the door to crack those coding interviews.
